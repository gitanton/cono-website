Conojo Whiteboard

A powerful whiteboard app for iPad.

Record, Collaborate, & Share In Real-Time

Conojo is an innovative drawing tool that makes it easier to share ideas, images, notes, and any other visual manifestation you can think of. From the technologically savvy to first time iPad users, Conojo makes anyone an artist or data wizard.

Record Live

Narrate and record your in-app brainstorming sessions and export them to social media and email in seconds.

Team Collaboration

Work live with colleagues, classmates, or friends to freely coordinate and communicate.

Easily Share

Seamlessly share your designs with friends and colleagues via email, Facebook, Twitter, Evernote, and more.

State of the Art Design Tools

Conojo is an all-in-one whiteboard where you can utilize drawing tools, templates, and data mapping to clearly convey your ideas with a team.




  • Work with a multitude of helpful tools for free hand drawing or creating unique concept maps.
  • Type up notes with over 100 stylish fonts.
  • Effortlessly import and export project files, compatible with all major file types.
  • Learn more with our numerous Training Videos and ‘Learn How to Draw’ Videos for beginners.

Convenient Templates

Stay efficient with our useful pre-made templates for quick brainstorming, note taking, or wire framing.


Visualize & Create with Conojo

Conojo has been designed for everyone from professionals such as architects, lawyers, creatives, sales professionals, engineers, and medical professionals to students, teachers, and parents.

Made for Business

Keep on track with your team. Collaborate and communicate live on projects with co-workers and clients.

Made for Education

Make learning a streamlined and fun process. Take detailed notes and share them with classmates, friends, and teachers.
